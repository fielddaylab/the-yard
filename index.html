About the Games

Field Day’s mini-games are 10-20 minute interactive science games for kids in grades 5-7 to explore, play, and experiment with science. Designed to make complicated systems easier to understand; the games are quick to set up and simple to play. They work in any modern browser. No complicated user accounts are required.

About the Yard

The mini-games are played in a delightful world inhabited by a ragtag bunch of kids. The relatable characters and creative setting help link the games together, establishing the purpose and real-world application for each game.
