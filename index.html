EXPLORABLE EXPLANATIONS

In addition to games, we are also making several explorable explanations. While games are fantastic as establishing a set of rules that establish a logical space for exploring, explorable explanations are more like a collection of simulators, woven together with some text that guide learners on a predefined, but interactive, path. A good EE will have the learner feel like they discovered something that was out of reach before exploring.
