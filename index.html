GAMES

Field Day's mini-games are 10-20 minute interactive science games for kids in grades 5-7 to explore, play, and experiment with science. Designed to make complicated systems easier to understand, the games are quick to set up and simple to play. They work on any device. No complicated user accounts are required.

EXPLORABLE EXPLANATIONS

In addition to games, we are also making several explorable explanations. While games are fantastic as establishing a set of rules that establish a logical space for exploring, explorable explanations are more like a collection of simulators, woven together with some text that guide learners on a predefined, but interactive, path. A good EE will have the learner feel like they discovered something that was out of reach before exploring.

BACTERIA!

When Po eats some nasty pizza he found in the garbage, Scout lays some education on him and Jack about the world of microbes.

PLAY!

ABOUT THE YARD

The mini-games are played in a delightful world inhabited by a ragtag bunch of kids. The relatable characters and creative setting help link the games together, establishing the purpose and real-world application for each game.
